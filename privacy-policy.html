Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Tileflux: Puzzle Blocks, published by Gustavo Guedes.

Overview

Tileflux is a puzzle game. The app does not require an account, does not ask for your name or email address inside the app, does not contain ads, and does not sell personal data.

Data We Collect

The app uses Firebase services provided by Google to understand basic app usage and diagnose crashes. These services may collect technical and usage information, including:

  • App interactions and gameplay-related usage events.
  • Crash logs, diagnostics, app performance data, and device/app information.
  • Device or installation identifiers used by Firebase.
  • Approximate location derived from network information, where provided by Firebase Analytics.

How We Use Data

Data is used to improve game stability, understand aggregate usage, diagnose crashes, and prioritize fixes. We do not use this data to identify individual players personally.

Third-Party Services

Tileflux uses the following Google Firebase services:

  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

Firebase data handling is described by Google in its own documentation and policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data processed by Firebase is handled by Google as a service provider for analytics, diagnostics, and crash reporting.

Children

Tileflux is not specifically directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us and we will review the request.

Data Deletion Requests

You may contact us to request deletion or ask questions about privacy. Some analytics and diagnostics data may be aggregated or stored by Firebase according to Google's retention policies.

Contact

For privacy or support requests, contact: gcguedesdev@gmail.com

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes, such as if cloud saves, Google Play Games features, or other online services are added.
