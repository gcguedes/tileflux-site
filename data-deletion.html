Data Deletion Request

For Tileflux: Puzzle Blocks, published by Gustavo Guedes.

How to Request Deletion

Tileflux does not require an account and does not collect your name, email address, or login credentials inside the app.

To request deletion of data associated with your app usage, email us at gcguedesdev@gmail.com with the subject line Tileflux Data Deletion Request.

If possible, include the approximate date when you used the app and the device platform, such as Android. Do not send sensitive personal information.

Data Covered

Requests may cover analytics, diagnostics, crash reports, device or installation identifiers, and approximate location data processed through Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics.

Data We Keep

Tileflux does not maintain player accounts or cloud save data at this time. Some analytics or diagnostics data may already be aggregated, anonymized, or retained by Firebase according to Google's retention policies and may not be individually identifiable or removable.

Processing Time

We will review deletion requests and respond within a reasonable period. If additional information is needed to process the request, we will reply to the email address used to contact us.
